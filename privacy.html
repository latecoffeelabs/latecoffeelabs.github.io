Privacy Policy

Late Coffee Labs · WeekSlayer for Android · Last updated: 17 May 2026

The short version

WeekSlayer does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. The app works entirely on your device. No accounts. No tracking. No ads. No analytics.

What data WeekSlayer stores on your device

The app stores the following information locally on your Android device using the system's standard preferences storage:

  • Your selected theme and personalization choices
  • Your work schedule, shift start/end times, and wage rate (if you use Shift Mode)
  • Your shift history and any notes you choose to save
  • Your story progress within the in-app narrative

This data never leaves your device. It is not sent to Late Coffee Labs or any third party. If you uninstall the app, this data is deleted by Android.

Permissions WeekSlayer requests

  • Notifications — to deliver shift reminders and end-of-shift alerts you have scheduled.
  • Exact alarms — to ensure shift reminders fire at the exact times you set, even when the device is idle.
  • Foreground service — to keep shift tracking accurate while you are actively on a shift.
  • Run at startup — to restore your scheduled reminders after your device reboots.

Third-party services

WeekSlayer does not use Firebase, Google Analytics, Crashlytics, advertising SDKs, or any other third-party SDK that collects or transmits data. The app makes no network requests.

Children's privacy

WeekSlayer is intended for users aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect any information from children, because we do not collect information from anyone.

Changes to this policy

If a future version of WeekSlayer ever adds features that involve data collection (for example, optional cloud backup), we will update this policy and request your consent before any data leaves your device.
